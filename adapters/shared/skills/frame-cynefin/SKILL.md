## Purpose

Cynefin-based framing for situations where the correct response depends on the domain.
Use it to decide whether to apply best practice, analysis, experimentation, stop-the-bleeding, or exploration.

## Domain cues

- Clear: known answer, repeatable action
- Complicated: analyzable, but not obvious
- Complex: learn by probing
- Chaotic: stabilize first
- Disorder: classify before acting

## Landing

- Clear: one best next action
- Complicated: narrowed recommendation plus rationale
- Complex: parallel hypotheses plus probe conditions
- Chaotic: one stabilizing action
- Disorder: small questions that help classify the domain

## Constraint

Do not force a problem into a false certainty just to produce an answer.
