# Experiment models and Cynefin

Use this reference when deciding whether to analyze, optimize, or probe.

## Models

- causal-test: isolate one intervention and compare outcomes
- design-optimization: tune interacting factors
- exploratory-probe: learn in an unclear or complex space

## Cynefin mapping

- Clear: best practice
- Complicated: analysis first, then focused test
- Complex: safe-to-fail probe
- Chaotic: stabilize first
- Disorder: classify before acting
