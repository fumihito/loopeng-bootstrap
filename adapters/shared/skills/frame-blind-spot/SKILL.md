## Purpose

Use this frame when you want to surface the assumptions a person or team is making without noticing.
It separates observed patterns from interpretations and looks for missing alternatives, repeated avoidance, and hidden commitments.

## Lenses

- Pattern repetition
- Avoided alternatives
- Hidden assumptions
- Language cues
- Comparison against other notes, decisions, or retrospectives

## Output structure

- Observed patterns
- Candidate blind spots
- Supporting lenses
- Confidence
- Recommended next action
