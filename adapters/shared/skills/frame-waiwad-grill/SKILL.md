## Purpose

Use this frame for incidents, errors, and retrospectives.
It avoids blame-first analysis and instead looks at the gap between how work was imagined and how it was actually done.

## Workflow

1. Accept the WAI/WAD gap.
2. Locate the kind of gap.
3. Trace the adaptation chain.
4. Grill the assumptions and reasoning.
5. Grill the constraints and incentives.
6. Redesign the conditions.

## Output structure

- WAI
- WAD
- Gap list
- Adaptation chain
- Root conditions
- Redesign ideas
