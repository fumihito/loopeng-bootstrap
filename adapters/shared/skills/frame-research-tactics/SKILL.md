## Purpose

Use this frame when research needs to turn into actionable tactics.
It helps build a hypothesis set, choose what to verify, and decide how to falsify bad ideas quickly.

## Workflow

1. Survey the relevant mental models or frameworks.
2. Group them into candidate hypotheses.
3. Choose the most promising hypotheses.
4. Define verification actions.
5. Define falsification actions.

## Output structure

- Framework survey
- Candidate hypotheses
- Top hypotheses
- Verification actions
- Falsification actions
- Next step
